I earned my DMA in 2018 from the Cleveland Institute of Music, where I studied under Keith Fitch and Steven Kohn. I'm currently on the faculty at CIM, where I'm the Director of Professional Skills and Breadth Development, Director of Electronic Music, and Theory and General Education Faculty. I've developed a variety of original classes in both theory and general education, including:

  • Contemporary Issues in Music
  • Music and Science Frontiers
  • Music in Culture
  • Philosophies of Music
  • Technology for the Working Musician
  • Physics of Music
  • The Experience of Hearing and Performance
  • History and Analysis of Music and Dance

My other work includes assessment, accreditation, and student academic success.
